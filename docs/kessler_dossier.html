Risk Controls — Hardwired in Software
  • GUARDIAN Independent kill-switch process. Physically severs broker connection if daily drawdown reaches the hard limit. Cannot be overridden by the trading engine.
  • KELLY CAP Position size is mathematically capped at a fraction of the Kelly-optimal bet. Prevents over-leveraging even if the model outputs an aggressive signal.
  • DUAL DD Monitors both daily drawdown (5% firm limit, 3% internal trigger) and maximum drawdown (10% firm limit, 5% internal trigger) simultaneously. Two independent gates.
  • NO HFT System operates on 1-minute bars, not tick-level arbitrage. Compliant with FTMO EA and execution policy.
⚠ What this report does not claim

This validation covers one month of out-of-sample data (4,800 ticks, 58 trades). It establishes statistical significance above the N=30 threshold and demonstrates a consistent edge against spread and commission. It is not a multi-year track record. Past performance does not guarantee future results. The model will be monitored continuously in live deployment and can be halted at any time by the Guardian process or by manual instruction. A losing month is possible and within the modelled risk parameters.
